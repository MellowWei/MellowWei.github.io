About / Mellow Wei

Philosophy asks what a system assumes. Design makes those assumptions tangible.

AI product designer · Learning-sciences researcher

I work at the intersection of human agency, interpretation, and emerging technology. My practice moves between rigorous inquiry and high-fidelity product design: understanding how people make sense of a situation, then shaping the interface around the judgment that situation requires.

My industry experience spans an AI translation product at NetEase Youdao, high-stakes travel journeys at Ctrip, and AI-assisted design tools at Lanhu.

I am currently pursuing an M.S.Ed. in Learning Sciences & Technologies at the University of Pennsylvania, after completing dual bachelor's degrees in Philosophy and Communication at the University of Southern California.

Design principle

Design should not only solve a problem. It should create a better relationship between people, information, and the decisions that follow.
